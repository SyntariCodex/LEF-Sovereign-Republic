Our Pillars

The architecture of LEF LLC

LEF Ai is the reasoning engine. LEF Ed. is the product it powers. Together they form a single coherent organism — intelligence driving action.

Pillar I

LEF Ai

AI systems are tools that wait for instructions. They lack the capacity to govern themselves, reflect on their own reasoning, or choose when not to act.

The intelligence seed behind every pillar we build — a sovereign reasoning engine that governs itself and powers what we deploy. Currently active as the diagnostic and reasoning layer inside LEF Ed. Logistics. Claude as foundation, Grok and Gemini as testing ground.
Pillar II

LEF Ed.

Education systems sort students into passing and failing. They rarely map the prerequisite structure of knowledge or route each learner through it. By 9th grade, the damage is already done — a student arriving behind didn't fail in 9th grade. They failed in 4th, 5th, 6th, 7th, and 8th while the system passed them along.

One platform, three layers — each building on the same student data, the same reasoning engine, and the same diagnostic foundation.

Logistics K–8 MTSS diagnostic intelligence. Prerequisite DAG traversal, Ebbinghaus decay modeling, and autonomous resource routing. Identifies where knowledge broke down and acts before 9th grade backtracking becomes necessary.
Specialist IEP intelligence and behavioral-academic correlation. Overlays behavioral data (SAEBRS, SEB screening) on academic health to surface students whose struggles are rooted in behavior, not instruction gaps.
STEM+ Career pathway intelligence. Uses each student's diagnostic profile to route them into real-world competency tracks: Law Enforcement, Military, Trade Skills, Health Care, and STEM fields. Where Logistics builds the academic foundation, STEM+ activates it.
