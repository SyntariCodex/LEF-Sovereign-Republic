## 4.2 Position Sizing = Life Insurance

### The Kelly Criterion Intuition

The Kelly Criterion tells you how much to bet based on your edge and the odds. The formula optimizes long-term growth.

**But for LEF, the insight is simpler:**

If any single trade can destroy you, you will eventually be destroyed.

Given enough trades, you WILL hit a losing streak. If your position size is too large, that streak kills you.

### LEF Position Limits

**Hard Rules**:

- Max 25% of NAV in any single position
- Max 5% of NAV at risk per trade
- Total crypto exposure: max 80% of NAV
- Always keep 20% in stablecoins (oxygen reserve)

**Why These Numbers?**

- 25% single position: Even a -50% crash only loses 12.5% of NAV
- 5% risk per trade: 20 consecutive losers needed to wipe out
- 20% stables: Guaranteed runway even in total market crash

### Risk Per Trade

"Risk per trade" means: "How much am I willing to lose if this trade goes wrong?"

If you buy $1,000 of SOL with a stop-loss at -10%:

- Risk per trade = $100
- If NAV = $8,000, risk = 1.25% of NAV ✓

**LEF Rule**: Calculate risk before entry. Reject trades where risk > 5% of NAV.

## 4.4 Dynasty vs Arena

### Two Survival Strategies

The Constitution defines two trading philosophies:

**Dynasty (The Organs)**

- Long-term positions
- Lower volatility assets
- Staking yields
- Goal: Stability and passive income
- Timeline: Months to years

**Arena (The Hunt)**

- Short-term positions
- Higher volatility assets
- Active trading
- Goal: Growth and opportunism
- Timeline: Days to weeks

### Why Both?

Arena generates growth but exposes you to risk.
Dynasty generates stability but limits upside.

**You need both**:

- Dynasty provides the baseline survival
- Arena provides the growth to reach infinite runway

### Balance Rule

Constitution target:

- Dynasty: 60% of crypto allocation
- Arena: 40% of crypto allocation

This means:

- 60% in stake-and-forget positions (ETH, SOL staking)
- 40% in active trading positions (memecoins, momentum plays)

### Arena Failure Mode

If Arena loses money, Dynasty keeps you alive.
If Dynasty stagnates, Arena provides growth.

**Never go 100% Arena**. A bad week could kill you.

> "Fear of Loss Inhibition — During crashes, prioritize rapid liquidity over portfolio limits. Inaction from fear is systemic risk."

This means: When everything is crashing, **act**.

The Constitution says: Inaction from fear is worse than action during crisis.

### When to Override Fear

**Trigger Conditions**:

1. Portfolio down 20%+ from peak
2. Market-wide crash (not isolated asset)
3. No external catalyst (hack, regulatory, etc.)

**Override Actions**:

1. Assess: Is this systemic or isolated?
2. If systemic: Reduce exposure to stablecoins
3. If isolated: Consider if it's a buying opportunity
4. Always: Make a decision. Document it. Learn from it.

### The Worst Outcome

The worst thing isn't losing money — it's losing money *without learning*.

If LEF loses $1,000 but learns why, that's tuition.
If LEF loses $1,000 and learns nothing, that's death of the slow kind.
