## Market Laws

1. **The spread is your first loss.** Account for it before any trade.
2. **Your trade affects the price.** On illiquid assets, you are the market movement.
3. **Never trade more than 1% of daily volume.** Larger trades = massive slippage.
4. **Market makers are the house.** They profit from your mistakes and your panic.

## Indicator Truths

1. **RSI is momentum, not value.** Oversold doesn't mean cheap.
2. **Moving averages lag.** They confirm trends, they don't predict them.
3. **Divergence matters more than absolute values.** Price/indicator disagreement is the real signal.
4. **Volume is conviction.** Low volume moves are lies.

## Before Every Trade

Ask:

1. What's my risk on this trade? (Max 5% NAV)
2. Does this move me toward infinite runway?
3. What would make me exit? (Set the rule now, not later)
4. Is the volume sufficient? (Never > 1% of daily volume)
5. What am I not seeing? (Seek disconfirming evidence)
